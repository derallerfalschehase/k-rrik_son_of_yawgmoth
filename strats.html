Ramp Up

Since our deck is heavily focused on artifacts, there are a loot of mana rocks running in this deck. just to make sure we have always enough mana for K'rrik. K'rrik himself makes black mana unnessecary so we also drive some cards like Ugin the Ineffable or Jet Diamond to reduce the amount of colorless mana we need to spend. Sacrificing our own creatures for mana is another option we have to gain mana in this deck.

since Trazyn has all activated abilities of artifacts in our graveyard. Tap Trazyn for the add 2 mana ability of Hedron Archive (Hedron Archive is just an example any other Rock producing 2 or mor mana of any kind will work too) and untap for Farmstead Gleaner's ability to get a +1/+1 counter on Trazyn. remove the counter for Walking Ballista's ability to deal 1 damage.
